2.1  $2x^{2}+3y^{2}+4z^{2}-5=0$
$$
\begin{aligned}
 & 2x^{2}+3y^{2}+4z^{2}=5 \\[6pt]
 & \frac{2x^{2}}{5}+\frac{3y^{2}}{5}+\frac{4z^{2}}{5}=1
\end{aligned}
$$
↑ эллипсоид.
![[Pasted image 20260209093552.png]]


2.2  $3x^{2}-4y^{2}+5z^{2}-6=0$
$$
\begin{aligned}
 & 3x^{2}-4y^{2}+5z^{2}=6 \\[6pt]
 & \frac{3x^{2}}{6}-\frac{4y^{2}}{6}+\frac{5z^{2}}{6}=1
\end{aligned}
$$
↑ гиперболоид одного листа
![[Pasted image 20260209093708.png]]


2.3  $4y^{2}+5z^{2}=6x^{2}-2$
$$
\begin{aligned}
 & -6x^{2}+4y^{2}+5z^{2}=-2 & |:(-2) \\[6pt]
 & 3x^{2} -2y^{2}-\frac{5}{2}z^{2} = 1
\end{aligned}
$$
↑ гиперболоид двух листов
![[Pasted image 20260209094156.png]]


2.4  $3z^{2}+2y^{2}-5x=0$
$$
\begin{aligned}
 & -5x=-3z^{2}-2y^{2} \\[6pt]
 & x=\frac{3}{5}z^{2}+\frac{2}{5}y^{2}
\end{aligned}
$$
↑ эллиптический параболоид
![[Pasted image 20260209094845.png]]
